Why Evidencer?

Evidencer is a complete investigative and courtroom solution that will change the way justice and legal proceedings are handled. Evidencer lets you record data, analyze data, search data and digitize physical evidences to provide you with a secure and reliable courtroom experience. Evidencer is a highly flexible, agile and fast application to transform any court into a complete digital courtroom. Evidencer was developed after extensive research of understanding how regular courts work, how police interrogations happen, what are the pain points and where digitization would make a huge difference. Utilizing new age technologies such as Enterprise Mobility, Enterprise Video Analytics and Big Data, SenzIT was able to create Evidencer as an end-to-end data solution for courtrooms, police interrogations and small courts.
