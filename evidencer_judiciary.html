Evidencer Suite for Judiciary

Evidencer Suite for Judiciary is an exceptional solution that is Enterprise-ready, support mobility and work in integration with the cloud platform. Thus it revolutionizes how the world executes a judiciary process. It comes packed with two enhanced products- the Evidencer Pro 4 channel which was created to capture evidence anywhere, anytime and make court proceedings simpler, less manual, trouble -free and digitized. The Judicial Evidencer suite also consists of the Evidencer Enterprise which delivers a high quality, efficient and effective approach towards the overall decision making and the justice process. One of the key differentiating product features is the live streaming of data & information using the product’s Central Broadcasting Server.

We have leveraged latest technologies like Big Data, Video Analytics, Enterprise Mobility and more to create an agile, robust and effective application.
