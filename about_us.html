Passion Makes Dreams Come True

SenzIT started in 2008 and has been a successful global provider of Information technology products, consulting and service provider since then. Spanning across the world in fastest growing business countries like Australia, UAE, Singapore and India; we are making the world a better place to live by “Sensing Information Technology”. IBM ASSOCIATION

Our partnership with IBM, the technology giant has given us a clear path for the upliftment of our product Evidencer. SenzIT along with IBM aims to complement our Justice system by providing exclusive video analytics, predictive analytics, business intelligence and so forth thus paving way for a connected justice. We are indeed fortunate enough in having a wonderful video analytics suite set integrated with our Evidencer thus raising the glory of our solution beyond sky limit. The video captured in courtrooms, police stations prisons can be played with and several conclusions can be derived after analyzing the data. Our Smarter Justice solution will outsmart all other solutions due to its capability to do Forecasting, i.e. to come out with forecast of the number of cases that would be expected to be presented in a court in a given day. The art of analyzing Video data automatically to detect temporal events would be a breakthrough in our product suite. The mighty Evidencer has the capability to do analytics in the court premises, which would be used for throwing up alerts in case of the number of people exceeding certain number, throw alert if any intrusion is there in a restricted area, or having abandoned objects.
