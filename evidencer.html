The Evidencer

Evidencer is a complete investigative and courtroom solution that will change the way justice and legal proceedings are handled. Evidencer lets you record data, analyse data, search data and digitize physical evidences to provide you with a secure and reliable courtroom experience.

Evidencer is a highly flexible, agile and fast application to transform any court into a complete digital courtroom. Evidencer was developed after extensive research of understanding how regular courts work, how police interrogations happen, what are the pain points and where digitization would makes a huge difference. Utilizing new age technologies such as Enterprise Mobility, Enterprise Video Analytics and Big Data, Senzit was able to create Evidencer as an end-to-end data solution for courtrooms, police interrogations and small courts.

Components

Capture

Capturing data in the form of audio, video and text is the crux of the Evidencer Suite. Experience seamless data capture and recording with EviMedia components that include the EviRecorder, EviPlayer and EviLogNotes. Capture data that ranges from small courts, police interrogation rooms to larger court proceedings.

Digital Strategy

Evidencer was created taking into account the Digital world that organizations are now embracing. Enterprise Mobility and Enterprise Analytics are key areas that organizations are looking to integrate in their day to day activities. With Evidencer, digitization of crucial activities in courtroom or police interrogation setup helps reduce any kind of tampering with proofs and capture events with accuracy to avoid falsification.

Multicam Recording

Advanced versions of Evidencer lets you install multiple cameras in strategic locations in a courtroom or other location of intent in such a way that every possible reaction or action of participants is captured. This means you have access to angles and body language from multiple camera feeds.

Enterprise Mobility

Mobility has changed the way data is stored, viewed and managed. What does Enterprise Mobility mean to an organization? With organizations that operate across the globes, with varied time zones, reducing redundancies and incorporating streamlines processes is a must. Employees are no longer attached to a desk; they are constantly on the go. Consider a sales consultant in your organization, with Enterprise Mobility, he is equipped to work on a presentation in the office, store it in your organization’s cloud storage and present it to the customer, at customer site, on his iPad. This entire scenario is not just restricted to employees, corporates in themselves are embracing this opportunity to be part of the Mobility revolution. Cloud platform, Big Data and Data Analytics can all work in tandem to provide you with means to monitor your organization’s business.

Store

Evidencer Mob integrates with leading databases that include Oracle, SQL and DB2 for storing captured audio, video and log notes. With broadcasting feature, Evidencer is powered with IBM Softlayer for cloud integration for broadcasting anywhere in the world, irrespective of location.

Cloud

Evidencer Mob provides flexibility to store captured data and on demand viewing due to its cloud integration. Powered with IBM Softlayer cloud, Evidencer can store large amount of captured data. IBM Softlayer ensures that sensitive information, when it comes to courts or police interrogation, is safeguarded and is securely stored.

Database Server

During case proceedings, the log notes are also taken by the clerical staff or appointed personnel. These textual files are stored in the database server along with URLs of the audio and video file location from the file storage server. The choice of database remains flexible with choices like Oracle, MS SQL and IBM DB2 to select from.

File Storage Server

When recording case hearings or police interrogations that are lengthier, the file storage server assists in storing these heavy data files. The storage server also stores attachments added during the recording session along with the actual recorded data securely.

View

Evidencer experience does not end at capturing data. With sophisticate player and broadcasting tools, Evidencer lets you stream captured video to multiple participants who can access the broadcast using their smartphones, tablets or desktops.

Search

Evidencer does not just record or capture audio and video data, but also lets you take log notes digitally. Each instance of recorded data is synched in perfection with the corresponding audio. Along with this, the log notes are synched with it too. Just search for an event from the recorded video and you can reach the event in less than half the time of a regular search.

Live Broadcasting

Share and broadcast live courtroom sessions or police interrogations that are being recorded with multiple cameras with ease. At just click of a button, transform your case hearing from a closed room hearing to an open air broadcast.

Video On Demand

Curate past court proceedings and make it available as video on demand. For future reference, participants and users can request for a video from a past hearing and revisit the hearing in no time.

Analytics

Data has transformed from being bits and bytes to volumes that are uncountable. This data needs to be mined effectively to derive conclusions that can help drive businesses. Analytics’ lets you analyse your data to help make better decisions and redirect your strategy towards profits.

BigData Text Analytics

With each case hearing and sessions, the amount of data that can be mined increases. Big Data lets you leverage and analyse voluminous quantities of data effectively to derive information such as criminal psychology, behavioural patterns and more even before case decisions are made. This means better judgements can be passed.

Facial Recognition

During court hearings and sessions, through Evidencer’s multi camera recording, video data is captured along with facial detection of every participant in the court. Take advantage of facial recognition technology powered by IBM I2 Coplink to map faces with stored data to reach conclusions faster.

Intelligent Video Analytics

Intelligent Video Analytics powered by IBM lets you perform advanced video analytics that assess behaviour and pattern through video analysis. Police and investigators are well equipped with insights and trend patterns that they can use to identify and draw conclusions better.

Report Generation

Any data analysis always needs qualitative report generation that helps in analysing pros and cons of a particular process. With reports that Evidencer generates, courtrooms and other processes can identify loopholes in processes and mitigate risks beforehand.

Web Portal

EviPortal provides authorized access to management tasks for other users who access the Evidencer software. Certain tasks and modules can be accessed only by a set of authorized users such as live streaming, broadcasting or generating reports. EviPortal lets you create separate groups for each of these tasks and provide users under that group with certain privileges. All tasks related to granting access and authorization can be controlled under EviPortal.
