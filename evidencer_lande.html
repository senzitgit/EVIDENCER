Evidencer Suite for Law and Enforcement

Evidencer suite for Law and Enforcement is an advanced investigation analysis and data recording system that captures and archives live proceedings from crime scenes to the primary investigation interviews at Police stations. It comes packed with two Evidencer products- the Evidencer Mob which is a mobile application that provides a single tap provision to the public by using their mobile device to record crime and reports the concerned or nearby crime department at anytime from anywhere straightaway. The Evidencer Pro 2 channel records the proceedings of a police station and keeps a record of reported FIRs.

Functionality
  • User can access the application with a simple first time verification of Email ID or mobile number.
  • With the tap of a button, the recorded audio/video data or captured image can be sent to the Police or crime department as an evidence.
  • User can edit the captured image or library image from the device as required.
  • Geolocation of the crime spot, date & time and subject or a brief textual detail are sent along with the recorded data.
  • Automated timestamp and location is tagged with the recorded data.
  • Application generates a reference number and loaded to the Police IT Server.
  • Information sent by the user is received by the Police control room which can be used to file an FIR straightaway and the user receives an acknowledgement in return.
  • A ‘Panic’ button is provided for quick connect; Police will know and have everything to pursue the verdict.
  • This data can also be integrated with Court case Management System to find any similar data previously existing.

Evidencer Mob is a real time application for instant crime reporting. It has one channel audio-video recording provision. Evidencer Mob provides a reporting platform for the common people through smartphones with fast, discrete and intelligent safety assistance. Crime-reporting is put literally into the hands of users for reporting an on-going crime to the crime department with the tap of a button instead of ignoring it. Evidencer Mob is integrated with IBM MobileFirst security features which is the prime purpose of the application.



Features Include
  • One channel audio / video recording.
  • Instant data collection.
  • App deployed in iOS / Android / Windows platform.
  • Real time and instant crime reporting.
  • User anonymity as per user requirement.
  • Instant transfer of the recorded data to crime department.
  • App delivered in an Evidencer Mobility platform.
  • Application accessible via internet or GSM

Evidencer Pro 2 Channel is an integrated software product that captures data like text, high quality audio and video and live events of a Police department on an enterprise mobility platform and lets you playback, whenever needed. Evidencer is a dedicated digital recording and content management solution for the society to earn their justice. Evidencer not only captures data, but analyses data by leveraging latest technologies like Big Data, and Enterprise Video Analytics.

It can digitally capture everything like the 24x7 activities along with FIR registered at Police stations and play them back. It has two channels of audio and video embedded. It allows transferring locally stored audio and video to the central server for future reference.
